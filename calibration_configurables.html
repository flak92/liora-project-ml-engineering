Hypothesis space vs proof standard

The one invariant that makes autonomy honest: a ladder version may vary the ADMISSIBLE hypothesis, but never the FROZEN proof standard. Now enforced field-by-field — rung_6_survivor_hpo is admissible, yet .alpha and .own_null.permutations are frozen leaves the guard rejects.

ADMISSIBLE — hypothesis

  • model_space ranges & hpo_trials
  • operating_point.grid (θ)
  • candidate families & arms
  • survivor-HPO budget_B
  • rung 7 interaction scope

FROZEN — proof standard

  • viability floor (split_nodes, pred_std)
  • cross-fit rules & acceptance statistic
  • α = 0.10 · permutation budget M = 50
  • block length & operating_point.mode
  • data boundary · OOS ban · certification

Terminal states — and who acts

Three of the four terminals are the machine finishing honestly. The fourth is the only place a human acts — the boundary of autonomy, made visible.

RESOLVED_RETAINED
MACHINE

a stable survivor was retained — the confirmed set.

RESOLVED_EMPTY
MACHINE

the evidence honestly supports no feature here. A result, not a failure — never hidden for a cleaner funnel.

LADDER_EXHAUSTED
MACHINE

every pre-authorized version walked, none added a confirmed feature. Stop.

NEEDS_CONTRACT
HUMAN

the current contract cannot proceed honestly. A person may author the next version (widening ADMISSIBLE only). Never an automatic loop side-effect.

Dependency order & the widen loop

Inside one outer fold the series calibrate in order — the feature set feeds HPO, HPO freezes, selection runs at frozen params, and the operating point θ is chosen last. A stuck rung never auto-widens: it stops and asks for the next hand-authored contract version.

Vocabulary. The ladder does human-pre-authorized hypothesis-space variation, not blanket "widening". The default θ move [0.75…0.99] → [0.80,0.90,0.95] is coarser / alternative, not a mathematical superset — "widen" is used only when the new range truly contains the old.
